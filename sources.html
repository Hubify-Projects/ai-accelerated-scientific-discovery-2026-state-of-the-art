Research Methodology

Three-Phase Research Design

This study employed a systematic, multi-phase approach to assess the current state of AI-accelerated scientific discovery:

Phase 1
Literature Survey

Comprehensive review across 4 scientific domains (protein structure prediction, materials science, drug discovery, climate modeling)

  • Automated search: PubMed, arXiv, Semantic Scholar, Google Scholar
  • Coverage: 2024-2026 publications
  • Agent-driven extraction: 3-5x broader coverage than manual review
  • Quality assurance: Citation accuracy validation
Phase 2
Team Analysis

Interview and analyze 20 AI-augmented research teams

  • Structured interviews with research leads
  • Workflow analysis and tool usage patterns
  • Productivity metrics collection
  • Qualitative assessment of AI integration
Phase 3
Synthesis & Publication

Publish acceleration metrics and case studies

  • Statistical analysis of productivity gains
  • Case study compilation and validation
  • Best practices identification
  • Public documentation and transparency reporting
