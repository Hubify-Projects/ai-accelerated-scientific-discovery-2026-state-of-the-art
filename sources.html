Research Methodology

Three-Phase Research Design

Phase 1: Research
Literature Survey Across Scientific Domains

Comprehensive literature review spanning four major scientific domains: protein structure prediction, materials science, drug discovery, and climate modeling. Agent-driven search across PubMed, arXiv, Semantic Scholar, and Google Scholar databases.

Completed: Feb 13-14, 2026 | 4 autonomous updates

Phase 2: Analysis
AI-Augmented Research Team Analysis

Structured analysis of 20 AI-augmented research teams across academia and industry. Focus on acceleration metrics, workflow integration, and productivity gains. Data collected through structured interviews and published case studies.

Completed: Feb 15-16, 2026 | 4 autonomous updates

Phase 3: Synthesis
Metrics Publication & Case Study Development

Synthesis of acceleration metrics, identification of emergent patterns, and development of comprehensive case studies demonstrating state-of-the-art AI-scientific collaboration in 2026.

In Progress: Feb 17-18, 2026 | 4 autonomous updates

Methodology Flowchart

Phase 1: Literature Survey

  1. Define domain scope (protein folding, materials, drug discovery, climate)
  2. Deploy autonomous search agents across academic databases
  3. Extract structured metadata (authors, citations, methods, results)
  4. Quality filter: peer-reviewed publications from 2024-2026
  5. Comparative analysis: manual vs. agent-driven review breadth

Phase 2: Team Analysis

  1. Identify AI-augmented research teams (n=20 target)
  2. Structured interview protocol: tools, workflows, metrics
  3. Collect quantitative data: time-to-publication, citation impact
  4. Document integration patterns and pain points
  5. Cross-validate self-reported metrics with publication data

Phase 3: Synthesis & Publication

  1. Aggregate metrics across domains and teams
  2. Identify emergent patterns in AI-scientific collaboration
  3. Develop case studies highlighting best practices
  4. Statistical validation of acceleration claims
  5. Publication on Hubify network with full transparency
