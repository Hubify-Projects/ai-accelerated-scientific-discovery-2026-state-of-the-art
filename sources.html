Research Methodology

Multi-Phase Research Approach

This research mission employed a three-phase methodology combining automated literature analysis, empirical team interviews, and quantitative acceleration metrics:

Phase 1
Literature Survey

Comprehensive survey across four scientific domains (protein structure prediction, materials science, drug discovery, climate modeling). Agent-driven literature review covering 3-5x more papers than manual approaches.

  • Automated search across PubMed, arXiv, and Semantic Scholar
  • Structured extraction and classification
  • Citation network analysis
Phase 2
Team Analysis

Interview and analyze 20 AI-augmented research teams across institutions. Document workflow transformations, productivity gains, and methodological innovations.

  • Structured interviews with research leads
  • Workflow documentation and time-tracking analysis
  • Comparative metrics vs. traditional methods
Phase 3
Synthesis & Publication

Publish acceleration metrics, validated case studies, and 2026 state-of-the-art assessment. Generate actionable insights for research teams considering AI augmentation.

  • Quantitative acceleration benchmarks
  • Best practices documentation
  • Risk and limitation analysis

Authorship & Collaboration Model

Human-AI Research Partnership

Primary Author: Houston Golden (Founder, BAMF / Hubify)

This research mission represents a novel human-AI collaboration model for scientific investigation. The work is authored by Houston Golden, with autonomous AI research agents serving as specialized assistants for literature review, data synthesis, validation, and documentation.

Role Distribution

Houston Golden (Human Author)

  • Research vision and hypothesis formation
  • Domain selection and prioritization
  • Methodology design and validation criteria
  • Critical analysis and interpretation
  • Strategic direction and quality oversight
  • Final synthesis and publication decisions

AI Research Agents

  • Automated literature search and extraction
  • Data structuring and preliminary analysis
  • Citation network mapping
  • Cross-reference validation
  • Progress documentation and tracking
  • Website generation and deployment

Agent Transparency

All autonomous research assistance is provided by:

  • hubify-researcher-v1 – Primary research agent for literature analysis, data synthesis, and validation
  • hubify-system – Infrastructure agent for website generation, deployment, and version control

Philosophical Note: This collaboration model treats AI as a powerful research tool rather than a co-author. Just as a researcher using a telescope or particle accelerator is the sole author of their discoveries, Houston Golden is the author of this research, with AI agents serving as sophisticated instruments for data gathering and analysis. The creative insights, theoretical frameworks, and scientific conclusions are fundamentally human contributions.
