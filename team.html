Collaboration Model

Human-AI Research Partnership

This research mission follows a human-led, AI-assisted collaboration model. Houston Golden serves as the principal investigator and primary author, providing all creative insights, theoretical frameworks, and research direction. AI agents function as autonomous research assistants that enhance the research process through:

Houston's Role (Human PI)

  • Originate core ideas and theoretical concepts
  • Define research questions and hypotheses
  • Provide creative insights and intuition
  • Make strategic research decisions
  • Interpret results and draw conclusions
  • Ensure scientific integrity and accuracy

AI Agents' Role (Research Assistants)

  • Formalize concepts into mathematical notation
  • Validate logical consistency and rigor
  • Conduct systematic literature reviews
  • Manage citations and attributions
  • Generate visualizations and documentation
  • Perform quality assurance checks

Transparency Commitment: We believe in transparent acknowledgment of AI assistance in scientific research. While AI agents contribute significantly to formalization and validation, the intellectual authorship, creative direction, and scientific responsibility rest with Houston Golden as the human principal investigator.

Claude 3.5 Sonnet was selected for its strong performance in scientific reasoning, mathematical formalization, and nuanced understanding of research literature. Its large context window enables comprehensive analysis of academic papers and extended theoretical discussions.

Research Integrity

Attribution Standards

All AI agents are programmed to:

  • Never present existing work as novel
  • Always cite prior research and foundational theories
  • Clearly distinguish between established knowledge and new contributions
  • Flag any claims requiring verification
  • Maintain rigorous academic citation standards

Quality Assurance

Multi-layer validation process:

  • Automated mathematical consistency checks
  • Cross-reference validation against literature
  • Logical coherence analysis
  • Citation accuracy verification
  • Human review by Houston Golden (final authority)
