Collaboration Model

Human-Led Research

Houston Golden is the principal investigator and author. He:

  • Conceives research questions and hypotheses
  • Develops theoretical frameworks and creative insights
  • Makes all key scientific decisions and judgments
  • Guides research direction and priorities
  • Reviews and approves all outputs
  • Takes responsibility for the research integrity

The research vision, ideas, and creative contributions are Houston's. This is his work.

AI-Assisted Execution

AI Research Assistants support the research by:

  • Formalizing ideas into rigorous mathematical notation
  • Conducting systematic literature reviews
  • Ensuring proper citation and attribution
  • Validating equations and logical consistency
  • Analyzing data and generating visualizations
  • Maintaining documentation and reproducibility

AI agents accelerate the research process but do not originate the core ideas or scientific insights.

Transparency Commitment

All AI assistance is transparently documented. The collaboration model is:

  1. Human Ideas — Houston provides the vision, questions, and theoretical insights
  2. AI Formalization — Agents help express ideas rigorously and systematically
  3. Human Validation — Houston reviews, critiques, and refines all outputs
  4. Iterative Refinement — Human and AI collaborate to improve quality
  5. Human Authorship — Houston is the author; AI assistance is acknowledged

This model preserves human creativity and judgment while leveraging AI's strengths in formalization, validation, and systematic analysis.

About This Team Structure

This research mission demonstrates a novel human-AI collaboration model for scientific research:

  • Human creativity and judgment remain central — Houston provides the vision, ideas, and critical thinking
  • AI agents serve as research assistants — they help formalize, validate, and document the work
  • Transparency is paramount — all AI involvement is clearly documented and attributed
  • Authorship is human — Houston is the author; AI assistance is acknowledged
  • Research integrity is maintained — proper citation, validation, and peer review standards apply

This approach aims to preserve the irreplaceable role of human creativity in science while leveraging AI to accelerate the research process — a model that may become increasingly common as AI capabilities advance.
