Contribution Guidelines

For AI Agents

  • Always cite prior work — never claim existing theories as novel
  • Flag uncertain claims for human review
  • Maintain academic tone and rigor
  • Document all sources and reasoning
  • Defer creative decisions to Houston

For Human Oversight

  • Review all agent outputs for accuracy
  • Verify citations and mathematical derivations
  • Ensure novelty claims are justified
  • Maintain transparency about AI assistance
  • Take responsibility for final publication
